üéØ How do I play Neo Run?
Neo Run is an endless runner game! Swipe left/right to move your cube, avoid obstacles, and collect coins. The game gets faster as you progress. Try to beat your high score!
üí∞ How do I earn coins?
Coins are collected during gameplay by running through them. You can also:
  • Purchase coin packs in the Store tab
  • Get daily bonuses with VIP Pass
  • Use Coin Doubler booster for 2x coins
üõçÔ∏è What can I buy with coins?
Use coins in the Shop to purchase:
  • Power-ups: Speed Boost (50 coins), Shield (75 coins), Coin Magnet (60 coins)
  • Skins: Customize your cube's appearance
  • Characters: Unlock different playable characters
  • Trails: Add visual effects to your cube
üîÑ How do I restore my purchases?
If you purchased "Remove Ads" or "VIP Pass" and reinstalled the app:
  1. Open the Store tab
  2. Scroll to the bottom
  3. Tap "Restore Purchases"
  4. Sign in with your Apple ID if prompted
Your non-consumable purchases will be restored automatically.
üö´ How do I remove ads?
Go to Store ‚Üí Premium section ‚Üí Purchase "Remove Ads" for $2.99. This is a one-time purchase that permanently removes all advertisements from the game forever.
üëë What is VIP Pass?
VIP Pass ($7.99/month) includes:
  • No ads
  • 2x coins permanently (all games)
  • Exclusive characters
  • Daily bonus coins (100 per day)
It's a subscription that renews monthly. Cancel anytime in iOS Settings.
üîê How do I cancel my VIP Pass subscription?
To cancel your subscription:
  1. Open iOS Settings
  2. Tap your name at the top
  3. Tap Subscriptions
  4. Select Neo Run - VIP Pass
  5. Tap Cancel Subscription
You'll keep VIP benefits until the current billing period ends.
üí≥ I was charged but didn't receive my purchase
First, try tapping "Restore Purchases" in the Store tab. If that doesn't work:
  1. Close and reopen the app completely
  2. Check your internet connection
  3. Verify the purchase in your Apple ID purchase history
  4. Contact us at hugomoriceau@icloud.com with your order details
All purchases go through Apple, so you can also contact Apple Support for payment issues.
üéÆ The game is lagging or crashing
Try these steps:
  • Close other apps running in the background
  • Restart your device
  • Update to the latest iOS version
  • Make sure you have the latest Neo Run version from the App Store
  • Try turning off some visual effects in Settings
If issues persist, email us your device model and iOS version.

üèÜ How do leaderboards work?
Leaderboards show your best score compared to other players. Your high score is automatically submitted when you beat your personal record. Leaderboards are updated in real-time.
