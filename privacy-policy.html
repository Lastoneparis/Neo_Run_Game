Welcome to Neo Run! This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile game application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a safe gaming experience.

Information We Collect

1. Game Progress Data

We collect and store information about your gameplay:

  • Current level and XP progress
  • Coins collected and balance
  • Unlocked skins and customizations
  • Daily mission progress
  • High scores and achievements

Storage: Stored locally on your device.

2. Purchase Information

When you make in-app purchases:

  • Purchase history (managed by Apple)
  • Transaction records

Note: Payment information is handled by Apple. We do not access your payment details.

3. Device Information

  • Device model and iOS version
  • Screen resolution
  • Language preferences

How We Use Your Information

  • Save your game progress
  • Process in-app purchases
  • Improve gameplay and fix bugs
  • Optimize performance
  • Provide customer support

Data Storage and Security

Your game data is stored locally on your device using secure iOS storage. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information.

Important: We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

Third-Party Services

Neo Run uses:

  • Apple StoreKit: For in-app purchases
  • Apple Game Center: Optional, for achievements

We do not use advertising networks or other third-party analytics services.

Children's Privacy

Neo Run is rated 4+ and appropriate for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

For Parents: No online features, no chat, no social media, no personal data collection. In-app purchases can be restricted via iOS Parental Controls.
